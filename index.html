With this online typing master, learning English typing is very easy. Just start with the keyboard displayed and rotate your fingers on the keyboard. Using a simple scientific method called this, we designed the typing master to learn fast, accurate and free typing. This typing course is absolutely free and a step-by-step guide for you. Simply practice 1 hour a day to become a professional typist. We recommend practicing 1 or 2 hours daily, this is not just a finger game, the mind plays an important role in keyboard learning.

So do not practice for 4-8 hours at a time but exercise a little daily.
